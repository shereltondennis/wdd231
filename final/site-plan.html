WDD231 Final Project Plan

Monrovia Food Finder

A planning document for a visitor-focused local dining guide for Monrovia, Liberia.

Site Name

Monrovia Food Finder

This name was selected because it clearly identifies the city and the main service of the site: helping visitors and residents find useful dining options in Monrovia.

Optional domain: monroviafoodfinder.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide a practical guide to restaurants, cafes, quick meal stops, and local food experiences in Monrovia. It will include a searchable dining directory, details about cuisine and neighborhood, and a simple form to help visitors plan a food visit.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a reliable Liberian lunch spot near Sinkor or Central Monrovia?
  • Which restaurants are best for seafood, coffee, or a casual family dinner?
  • How can I request a simple dining plan for my visit date and group size?
